Support

LIVENOTE is an iOS notes app that lets you check notes and links directly from the Lock Screen.

Last updated: February 24, 2026

You can quickly save today's tasks, important schedules, and links to read later, then view them right from the Lock Screen.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Which iOS versions are supported?

A. LIVENOTE supports iOS 17 and later.

Q. Is my data sent to external servers?

A. LIVENOTE does not send your notes or links to external servers and manages data only on your device.

Q. What happens to data after reinstalling the app?

A. Notes and links saved with Archive are stored in your personal iCloud account and can be restored after reinstalling.

Privacy Notice

LIVENOTE handles personal data with the following principles.

  • No separate account registration is required.
  • Notes and links are stored on-device, and the developer does not collect or analyze that content.
  • Personal information is not shared with third parties.

If you have additional privacy questions, contact us at the email above.
