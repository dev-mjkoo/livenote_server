This policy explains how LiveNote handles data and protects user rights.

Last updated: February 24, 2026

1. App Information

Contact: help@lvnt.my

2. Data We Collect

The app may collect anonymous statistics through Firebase Analytics.

  • Collected items: app usage patterns (feature usage, frequency, etc.)
  • Purpose: service improvement and better user experience
  • Important: no personally identifiable information is collected
  • Storage: Google Firebase servers

3. User Choice

You can disable analytics data collection at any time in app settings.

4. iCloud Synchronization

Notes, links, and categories are synchronized through Apple iCloud.

  • The developer cannot access personal data stored in iCloud.
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to that data.

5. Legal Compliance (GDPR and Korean PIPA)

LiveNote complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Korean Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

You have the following rights:

  • Request access to and correction of personal data
  • Request deletion of data
  • Request restriction of or objection to processing
  • Withdraw consent for data collection

You may submit requests through the developer contact channels.
